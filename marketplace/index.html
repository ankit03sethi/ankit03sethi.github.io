FAQs

Quick answers

Which marketplaces do you support?

Amazon India, Flipkart, Meesho. (Also Myntra, Firstcry, JioMart on request — pricing varies.)

Do I need GST first?

Yes - all 3 marketplaces require a valid GSTIN. If you don't have GST yet, start with our GST Registration (₹999/-) first.

Can I open accounts on multiple platforms?

Yes - ₹999/-. Most sellers start with Amazon + Meesho (different customer bases). Tell us during the callback.

What if the marketplace rejects my application?

We resubmit with corrections at no extra cost. If a category requires special approval (e.g. Amazon's brand registry), we co-ordinate that.

Are listing fees included?

No - our ₹999/- covers account opening. Marketplaces' own listing/commission fees are separate and paid directly to them per sale.

Can I cancel and get a refund?

Yes — full refund with of payment if we haven't started the account opening process.
