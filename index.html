FAQs

Quick answers

I'm just starting out. Where should I begin?

Start with GST registration if you plan to sell on any marketplace — it's mandatory. Pair it with Udyam for govt scheme access. Then open your seller account once GSTIN is in hand.

Do I really need a trademark before launching?

Not legally, but yes practically. Without a registered trademark, competitors can list under your brand name and you can't stop them. We recommend filing it the moment your brand starts seeing real sales.

How is the analytics dashboard different from Amazon Seller Central?

Seller Central shows you only one marketplace at a time. Our dashboard shows you all your marketplaces in one view, updates regularly, and flags when your listings go down — even before the marketplace notifies you.
