FAQs

Quick answers

Do I need GST to sell on Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho or Myntra?

Yes. All major Indian marketplaces require a valid GSTIN to onboard sellers. Without it, your seller account cannot be approved.

What is your turnaround time really?

Most GSTINs are issued by the department with of payment if your documents are complete. In rare cases (eg. document re-verification by the officer), it may extend to 24-48 hours.

Are government fees included in the ₹999/-?

₹999/- covers our service charge + GST. No extra payments. All-inclusive.

What if my application gets rejected by the GST officer?

We refile at no extra cost. If the officer requests additional documents, we co-ordinate with you and resubmit. Service fee is not charged twice.

Can I cancel and get a refund?

Yes Yes — full refund if you cancel with of payment AND we haven't yet submitted the filing. Once submitted to the GST department, government fees are non-refundable. I'm a freelancer / home-business / not a registered company. Can I still get GST?

Absolutely. Proprietorship (sole owner) GST takes the same time and costs ₹999/-. No company registration needed.
