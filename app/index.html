Getting started

A few things you can do in your workspace right away.

  1. Install the cursive Chrome extension (coming to the Web Store) and log in — it carries your identity automatically.
  2. Add product URLs you want to track in Products.
  3. Upload Flipkart / Amazon / Meesho sales reports in Files — they auto-clean into your master sheet.
  4. Check Master data for your unified order book and P&L.
  5. Watch Alerts for listing suppressions, price drops and low stock.
