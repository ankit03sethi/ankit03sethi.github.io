FAQs

Quick answers

How long does the full trademark registration take?

Full registration (i.e. the ® mark) takes 12-18 months from the Trademark Registry's side. But you can use ™ immediately after filing - it's legally recognised.

What's the difference between ™ and ®?

™ means you've applied for trademark and claim the brand. ® means it's been formally registered. You can use ™ from day one after filing with us.

What if someone else has the same name?

We do a free pre-filing search before payment to flag conflicts. If your name clashes, we'll discuss alternatives with you before charging.

Are government fees included?

Yes. ₹6,999/- covers the ₹4,500/- govt filing fee + our service charge + GST. No additional payments asked.

Can I cancel and get a refund?

Yes — full refund if you cancel with AND we haven't filed with the Registry. Once filed, government fees are non-refundable. Service fees reviewed case by case.

What if the Registry objects to my application?

We handle a first response to objections at no extra cost. If a hearing is needed, that's a separate engagement we'll quote transparently.
