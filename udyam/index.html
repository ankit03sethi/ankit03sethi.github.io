FAQs

Quick answers

What is Udyam and why do I need it?

Udyam is the Govt of India's MSME registration. It officially classifies your business as Micro / Small / Medium and gives you access to schemes like MUDRA loans, lower bank interest rates, govt tender bidding, and procurement preference.

Isn't Udyam free on the govt portal?

Yes, the portal charges nothing. ₹999/- is our service fee for getting it done correctly - right NIC code, right classification, no rejections. Many DIY attempts fail due to mismatch with Aadhaar/PAN data.

How long does it take?

Most certificates issue with of payment. In rare cases (Aadhaar mismatch), we co-ordinate with you and resubmit - takes 2-3 days.

Do I need GST first?

No. Udyam can be obtained independently. Many businesses get Udyam BEFORE GST to access startup-friendly schemes.

Can I cancel and get a refund?

Yes — full refund with of payment if not yet filed. After filing, the govt fee is non-refundable.
