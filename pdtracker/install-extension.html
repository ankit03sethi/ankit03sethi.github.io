Option 1 — Auto-install (recommended)

One file. One double-click. No typing email/password. Auto-installs everything.
⬇ Download Installer (.bat)
  1. Click the download button above (4 KB file).
  2. Double-click the downloaded CursivePDTracker-Setup.bat in your Downloads folder.
    (If Windows warns "Windows protected your PC" → click "More info" → "Run anyway". This is normal for new installers.)
  3. Press Enter when the installer window opens. It takes ~30 seconds: downloads the extension, sets Chrome to auto-launch on PC boot, opens Chrome.
  4. In Chrome (just opened): toggle Developer mode ON (top-right), click Load unpacked, press Ctrl+V to paste the path, then Enter.
  5. Open cursive.world/pdtracker/ — the extension auto-signs you in. Done! Green toast "Extension connected!" appears at the bottom-right.

After install, Chrome auto-launches every time you turn on your PC. The extension runs silently in the background — you never need to do this again.
