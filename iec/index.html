FAQs

Quick answers

Do I need IEC to sell on Amazon US / Etsy / international platforms?

Yes. Any cross-border commerce from India requires an IEC. Without it, banks won't process your foreign currency receipts and customs won't clear your shipments.

Is IEC the same as GST?

No. GST is for domestic taxation. IEC is for cross-border trade. If you sell only within India, GST is enough. If you sell abroad, you need BOTH.

How long is the IEC valid?

Lifetime. Once issued, IEC never expires - you just need to keep your bank and address details updated.

Can I get IEC without GST?

Yes. IEC is independent of GST. You can apply for IEC alone if you only do export business and are below the GST threshold.

Can I cancel and get a refund?

Yes — full refund with of payment if not yet filed. After filing, govt fee is non-refundable.
